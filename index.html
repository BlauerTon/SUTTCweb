About SUTTC

The Strathmore University Table Tennis Club (SUTTC) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant table tennis community. We organize events, host competitions, and encourage skill development in a friendly atmosphere. We host the annual SUTTC competition which brings people from all over Kenya for the love of Table Tennis. The games are inclusive of singles, doubles, and the much-loved family games where families get to compete against each other.
